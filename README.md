**Neon Bloom** is a 2D top-down survival game set in a futuristic, high-tech orbital station.  
You play as an android gardener, exploring, collecting resources, and growing techno-organic plants to survive, evolve, and uncover the secrets of a dying station.

---

## ðŸŽ® Gameplay

- **Explore** procedurally designed station sectors.
- **Collect resources**: organic, synthetic, and data.
- **Grow hybrid flora** to generate food, energy, and upgrades.
- **Survive** by managing Power, Integrity, and Memory.
- **Evolve** by integrating plant traits into your chassis.
